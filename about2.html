hi,I'mBryant.

I’m an interaction designer who does not satisfied with screens. Who love systematic challenges and enjoy 0 to 1 more than 1 to 100. Who thinks design is more about creating meaning than consumption, and who uses it as a tool to sparks people, create changes they want to see in the world.

I’m a designer who is best when I surrounded by passionate people. So I joined IDEO for the past two years, using design to inspire China’s business leaders to explore opportunities while creating impacts in scale. Before, I was in Mozilla, helping the non-profit to keep the internet remain open and secure. We crafted Firefox to be a modern browser and bring web experience on TV and mobile to the southeast users. Prior to that, I was experimenting with cutting-edge technology in HTC lab, creating innovative wellness solutions across mobile, wearable, and IoT. Back in school, I studied service and interaction design, but my gig is still making things tangible, a place where I started.

When I’m free, I love to get lost in the places where I find interesting links between bits and atoms, and how it can do good in our everyday life.
