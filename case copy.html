HUAZHU

Reimagining hotel digital experience in scale

Interaction Design, Service design

With 100 million users and growing, Huazhu is building its hotel empire in China. Despite being the digital first-mover, its app has stumbled by its complex legacy and indifferent offerings. It is time to reinvent itself to sustain in China's competitive marketplace.

Challanges

Huazhu group is the 2nd largest player in China’s hotel industry. Today, the company has over 3000 hotels and 500,000 rooms in the country alone. The mobile app, which is the one-stop shop for all its core business - including hotel sales, membership program, e-commerce, and more, are facing server challenges from OTAs and competitors, for their cheap pricing and good user experiences.

Bearing the threat, the leadership team comes to us with one ask: how might we reinvent the app experience in both aesthetic and offerings, that is differentiated and true to the company’s brand. In this project, I lead the team with close collaboration with the clients, setting up research and design direction as well as the app redesign and production.

Approach

Using design to inspire strategic decisions

Kickoff

Bridging the gaps between silos

Since the app is own by different business units, we kicked off the project by conducting a series of internal interviews to understand the needs, interests, and expectations from all leaderships and functional teams, including product, sales, service, and brand.

We audit the app to breakdown the major features while analyzing data from the current design and its competitors to learn what has been tried and how they perform. These efforts help us to onboard quickly and gather consensus on key areas for the design to explore down the line.

Reduce the complexity of the app as it suffers the experience and performance

Enhance the service and membership experience so Huazhu can play its strength

Create a refreshing visual language to advocate what Huazhu stands for

Research

Dipping ourselves in the user’s shoe

The primary user of the Huazhu app is business travelers, which consist of over 60% of the traffic. So we start our research by conducting interviews with the target group, to learn more about their pain points and needs, and what their journey looks like.

During the session, we use low-fidelity prototypes with a co-creation exercise to facilitate conversation, collect potential ideas, and more importantly the ‘why’. We also interviewed two experts who have rich experience in membership programs and hotel services to draw inspiration from their wide exposure and edge use cases.

Finally, to put ourselves in the shoes of the user, we immerse ourselves in several Huazhu’s hotels to learn how the online and offline service operates, and how it feels as a customer.

With the economic price in mind, the customer did not expect prime service, but a touching one that brings instant value to them.

Online and offline service is poorly integrated. The competing relationship not only confused the user but also fail to bring out the best value.

The membership program is too complicated and overly salesy. It diminishes the trust and motivation for people to engage.

Design

Identify the core offering through rapid prototyping

With the rich insight in hand, the team is ready to get the design running. To make the process more efficient, we first map out all the features and content blocks of the app, divided them into four independent experience modules - first entry, search, service, membership, and tackle them separately.

The workflow enables us to builds quick prototypes on a daily basis to test and discuss with the client. It also helps us integrate better with the product team, raises health tension between different functional teams to decide what is really matters to their business and brand. With runs of prioritization, a core feature set has emerged that plays to Huazhu’s strength and meets its user’s needs.

Creating a visual language that stands out

One focus for the project is to use the app as a medium to showcase what Huazhu means as a brand to stand out in the China market. So, along with the feature conversation, we also collected a set of mood boards with different art directions and applied it to key screens to spark conversations with the leadership team to capture the essence of the vision - to provide affordable hotel service with integrity and elegance. The result is an image-rich, minimalist visual language that celebrates content with nature and human touch.

Building a robust design system that scale

We knew from day one that this app needs a robust design system to grow in scale. It’s not only because it intertwines complex information with huge traffic, but also because the product belongs to a variety of businesses with different work approaches and interests. Therefore, once the key screens are in place, we start to audit all the common components, align them with the new visual identity, and transfer them into a set of guidelines and resources. The deliverable helped the in-house team to work in parallel on other parts of the app while keeping high consistency with performance.

Outcome

Throughout the project, the Huazhu team keeps expressing how they have benefited from the learning of the process. The product team is equipped with new work approaches and the leadership team is much confident of who they are, and how to play to their strengths to serve the customers. With the app’s 110 million users, we are about to see a refreshing digital experience that impacts the China hospitality industry in early 2021.

Final Design

The close collaboration brings fruitful results. Within just 7 weeks we have reconstructed the app’s content structure, identified a set of key features that play well with Huazhu's strengths. We also create a new visual system that captured Huazhu's brand image. The new app blends online and offline services, providing an affordable, personalized experience to meet the business and recreation needs of Chinese customers.

01

Fast & slow modes

Fast on tasks

Users come to the hotel app with tasks in mind. If they feel lost, they will drop it right away. This is even more so for business users. We prioritized key information on first fold to keep the experience handy and smooth.

Slow to explore

Huazhu has a lot of great content and programs that are unknown to its users. We surface those information by making it more discoverable and appealing, so users can enjoy and learn about the brand when they have time.

Promotion as content

Glaring ads are common scenes in China’s hospitality app. While this might increase sales, it creates stress and distrust. We instead turn the promotions into a set of contextual content cards, so users find it more useful and stand out from the competitors.

02

One service portal

Online & offline

Offline services are a key advantage for Huazhu, but they are not well integrated with the online platform. We blend the two into one service portal, so the users can take better control of their trip while getting the most out of their purchase.

Featured service

Many hotels under Huazhu have their unique services (ex. local breakfast), we celebrate the offering by creating a set of feature cards that shows up contextually, so users have a better chance to discover and enjoy them.

One service tap

The speed to cover problems is key to customer satisfaction. We created a service tab that quickly matches needs with the right representative. Helping Huazhu to better connect with its customers.

03

Maximizing membership value

Clear status and benefits

Many Huazhu members don’t know about their membership status and benefits. We strived to highlight the information by using simple language and visuals to help them understand the value in a glance. Hence increase trust and engagements.
